Refactored 2026-04-10 from a JS-positioned floating .nav-notch system to a pure-CSS per-button ::before. The previous clip-path approach was rejected because it overrides border-radius, producing a square artifact on the rounded left edge. The mask-image + mask-composite: add approach keeps border-radius intact on the body half while a triangular SVG mask handles the tip — everything paints in a single pseudo with one continuous vertical gradient, so there's no seam where body meets tip.
