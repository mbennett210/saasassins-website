PolishPoint — Killed Five SaaS Tools.

An all-in-one operations platform for cleaning service companies. Scheduling, CRM, invoicing, GPS clock-in, field ops, branded website, customer portal — one system, customer-owned, fraction of the monthly cost.

Industry: Cleaning Services

The All-in-One Platform Cleaning Operators Actually Own

PolishPoint replaces the typical stack — Jobber, HubSpot, QuickBooks, T-Sheets, a branded WordPress site — with one coherent product owned by the cleaning company. Native mobile app for the field crew. Dashboard for the office. Customer portal for the clients.

React Next.js TypeScript Supabase PostgreSQL React Native Expo Stripe Twilio Resend Vercel
⚔ The Problem

Cleaning operators were stitching together 4–5 SaaS tools — each $100–500/month — none of which talked to each other. Data lived in spreadsheets. Field teams had no real-time schedule. Quotes went out on PDFs. Tool sprawl was eating margin and operator time.

⚔ The Solution

A single custom platform replacing the entire stack. Office dashboard, tablet schedule view, native mobile app with GPS clock-in and photo capture, integrated Stripe invoicing, SMS/email through Twilio & Resend, branded customer booking portal. Deployed to the client's Vercel + Supabase.

⚔ The Result

Five SaaS subscriptions cancelled. One-time build paid for itself in month 8. Operator owns every line of code, every row of data. Entire business can run off a single platform — or be extended by any React developer, forever.
