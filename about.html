Our Story

We Got Tired of Paying Rent on Software.

Every business we'd worked with was bleeding cash on the same stack — CRM, scheduling, invoicing, field ops, HR — each $100 to $500 a month, each owned by someone else, each one patch release away from breaking a critical workflow.

The worst part: 80% of the features went unused. The 20% that mattered could've been a custom build that paid for itself in under a year.

So we started doing it. One business at a time. No monthly fees. Clean code. Real ownership. Turns out, once you show an operator what custom-built actually costs versus what they've been paying, the decision takes about ten minutes.
