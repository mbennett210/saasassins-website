Program & Sender Identity

The sender of all messages under this program is SaaSassins, LLC, a software development studio. SaaSassins builds custom software that businesses own outright through a one-time fee — including PolishPoint, our productized, one-time-fee software deployment.

Text messages are dispatched through our SMS service provider, Twilio, under an A2P 10DLC (application-to-person) registered messaging program. Messages will identify SaaSassins as the sender. Questions about the program can be directed to hello@saasassins.com.

Program Description & Message Types

If you opt in, SaaSassins will send you recurring automated text messages related to your account and your project. These messages may include:

  • Onboarding updates — status of your build, setup steps, and information we need from you to provision and brand your instance.
  • Account and service notifications — important updates about your deployment, delivery, and ongoing maintenance.
  • Support communications — responses and follow-ups related to help requests you raise with us.

This is a customer-care and account-notification program. It is not a marketing or promotional advertising program.

How to Opt In

You opt in to the SMS program after you complete checkout. On the post-checkout intake form, check the SMS consent box and make sure the phone number you enter in the form’s support phone field is a mobile number that can receive text messages. The SMS program uses that same support phone number — there is no separate text-messaging number field. The consent language on the form reads:

“Text me updates: I agree to receive recurring automated text messages from SaaSassins at the phone number above (onboarding updates, account notifications, and support). Consent is not a condition of purchase. Message frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help.”

The SMS box is unchecked by default and sits apart from the other intake fields. You must affirmatively check it to enroll, and you should provide a text-capable mobile number so that messages can reach you. Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of purchasing any SaaSassins product or service, and you can complete your purchase and onboarding without opting in. By checking the box and providing a number, you confirm that you are authorized to receive text messages at that number.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. The number of messages you receive depends on the stage of your onboarding, your account activity, and any support requests you make. There is no fixed number of messages per period.

Costs

SaaSassins does not charge a fee to participate in the SMS program. However, message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and your plan. You are responsible for any such charges. Contact your wireless carrier for details about your messaging and data plan.

How to Opt Out

You can cancel the SMS program at any time. Reply STOP to any message you receive from us. After you send STOP, you will no longer receive automated text messages from this program; you may receive a confirmation that you have been unsubscribed.

If you want to rejoin later, you can opt in again by submitting the intake form with the SMS box checked, or by contacting us at hello@saasassins.com.

Help

For help with the SMS program, reply HELP to any message and you will receive information on how to get support. You can also email us at hello@saasassins.com at any time with questions about the program.
